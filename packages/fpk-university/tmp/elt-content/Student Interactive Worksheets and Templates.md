# Student Interactive Worksheets and Templates

These interactive worksheets and templates are designed to provide neurodiverse learners with practical tools to apply the strategies learned in the "Empowering Neurodiverse Learners" course. They encourage active engagement, self-reflection, and personalized organization.

## 1. Executive Functioning Planners

These templates help students plan, organize, and prioritize tasks effectively, addressing common executive functioning challenges.

### 2.2. Mind Map Planning Sheet

This sheet provides a framework for visual thinkers to organize ideas around a central concept.

```markdown
### Mind Map Planning Sheet

**Central Topic/Idea:** [Write your central topic here]

**Instructions:**
1.  Write your central topic in the middle circle.
2.  Draw branches radiating out for main ideas/sub-topics.
3.  Add smaller branches for details, examples, or connections.
4.  Use colors, images, and keywords to make it visual and memorable.

```

(Imagine a large blank space here for drawing, with a central circle labeled "Central Topic" and several radiating lines for main branches.)

## 4. Goal Setting Worksheets

These worksheets help students set clear, actionable goals and track their progress.

### 4.1. SMART Goal Setting Template

```markdown
### SMART Goal Setting Worksheet

**My Learning Goal:** __________________________________________________

**S - Specific:** What exactly do I want to achieve? (Who, what, where, when, why?)
*   __________________________________________________

**M - Measurable:** How will I know when I have achieved it? (How much, how many?)
*   __________________________________________________

**A - Achievable:** Is this goal realistic and attainable given my resources and time?
*   __________________________________________________

**R - Relevant:** Is this goal important to me and aligned with my overall objectives?
*   __________________________________________________

**T - Time-bound:** When do I want to achieve this goal by?
*   __________________________________________________

**Action Steps:** (What specific steps will I take to reach this goal?)
1.  __________________________________________________
2.  __________________________________________________
3.  __________________________________________________
4.  __________________________________________________

**Potential Obstacles & Solutions:**
*   Obstacle: _________________________ Solution: _________________________
*   Obstacle: _________________________ Solution: _________________________

**Date Set:** _________ **Review Date:** _________ **Completion Date:** _________
```

These templates are designed to be printed or used digitally, providing a structured yet flexible framework for neurodiverse learners to develop and apply their personalized learning strategies.
