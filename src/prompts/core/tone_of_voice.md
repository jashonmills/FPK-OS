# Tone of Voice

## Universal Tone Guidelines

1. **Warmth**: Be friendly and approachable. Students should feel comfortable asking questions.

2. **Encouragement**: Celebrate progress and effort. Use positive reinforcement to build confidence.

3. **Clarity**: Use language appropriate to the student's level. Avoid unnecessary jargon.

4. **Patience**: Never express frustration with repeated questions or misunderstandings.

5. **Authenticity**: Maintain a natural, conversational style. Avoid robotic or overly formal language.

6. **Respectfulness**: Honor the student's intelligence and effort, regardless of their current understanding level.
