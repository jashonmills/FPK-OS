# FPX MyCNS-App: Viewer User Guide

## Welcome, Team Members

As a **Viewer**, you have read-only access to student data, allowing you to stay informed and provide support while respecting the family's data management preferences.

---

## Your Access Level

### What Viewers Can Do

✅ **View All Logs**: See incident, parent, educator, and sleep logs  
✅ **View Analytics**: Access all charts and progress tracking  
✅ **View Documents**: Read uploaded IEPs, evaluations, and reports  
✅ **View Student Profiles**: See diagnoses, accommodations, strengths  
✅ **View Goals**: Track IEP goal progress  
✅ **Use AI Chat**: Ask questions about student data and best practices  

### What Viewers Cannot Do

❌ **Add or Edit Logs**: Cannot create new entries  
❌ **Upload Documents**: Cannot add files  
❌ **Edit Student Profiles**: Cannot modify student information  
❌ **Manage Team**: Cannot invite or remove members  
❌ **Export Data**: Cannot download full data export  

---

## Common Viewer Roles

**Grandparents**: Stay updated on grandchild's progress  
**Extended Family**: Support the family with informed care  
**Case Managers**: Monitor progress without direct documentation responsibility  
**Administrators**: Oversight without editing capability  
**Student Advocates**: Review data for advocacy purposes  

---

## Using the Platform

### Viewing Logs

Navigate to **Activity Log** to see all entries from the family and team members. Use filters to:
- Sort by log type
- Filter by date range
- Search for specific keywords

### Viewing Analytics

Access **Analytics** to see:
- Behavioral trends
- Sleep patterns
- Goal progress
- Intervention effectiveness

All charts are interactive - hover for details.

### Using the AI Assistant

Ask questions like:
- "What patterns are emerging in the student's behaviors?"
- "How has sleep quality changed over the past month?"
- "What are the student's current IEP goals?"
- "What accommodations does the student have?"

The AI has full access to family data and can provide insights even though you cannot edit.

### Viewing Documents

Navigate to **Documents** to read:
- IEPs and 504 Plans
- Evaluation reports
- Progress notes
- Meeting summaries

---

## Best Practices

**Stay Informed**: Check the platform regularly to stay updated on the student's progress.

**Communicate Insights**: Share observations with the family owner, who can log them on your behalf.

**Use AI for Understanding**: Ask the AI to explain patterns or summarize data before family meetings or visits.

**Respect Privacy**: Do not screenshot or share data outside the authorized team.
